Got Questions

Common questions
answered honestly.

Is Covenant Two free to use?

Yes. Covenant Two is completely free to use right now. Open the app, set up your profile, and start your first session today with no payment required.

Do both partners need to be on the app at the same time?

No. Couple sessions are designed for one phone, passed between both partners in the same room. Solo modes let each person use the app independently. No account syncing or separate logins needed.

Is this only for Christian couples?

Covenant Two is built on a biblical foundation and uses Scripture throughout. It is designed for couples who want to bring God into their marriage. If that resonates with you, you will find a home here.

How does the AI work?

The app uses an AI to generate devotionals, prayer points, and questions based on your topic and profile answers. Think of it as a pastoral writing assistant — it creates something personal and Scripture rooted for your specific situation every single time.

Is my data private?

All your session data, prayers, and profile answers are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers beyond the AI prompt needed to generate your devotional. Your private conversations stay private.

Can my pastor or counselor recommend this?

Absolutely. Covenant Two is designed to complement professional counseling and pastoral care, not replace it. It gives couples a meaningful daily rhythm between sessions. Share the app link with your congregation or clients directly.
